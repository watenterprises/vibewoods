Shibu Resident

30 X 40

JH Group is dedicated to delivering excellence in every project. With a team of skilled professionals and a commitment to client satisfaction, Shibu Resident exemplifies the highest standards of quality, innovation, and customer service.

Shibu Resident is a distinguished project by JH Group, situated in Hessarghatta Tharamarasanahalli. This residential development boasts a 30*40 site dimension, offering a well-planned and thoughtfully designed living space. With JH Group's expertise in floor planning, structural design, and interior aesthetics, Shibu Resident promises a blend of functionality and elegance.

The development features innovative floor plans, robust structural design, and elegant interiors, ensuring optimal space utilization and modern living comfort. With advanced 3D elevations, residents can visualize their dream home before construction. The serene location offers a peaceful environment and convenient access to major roads and amenities. JH Group’s commitment to quality and client satisfaction shines through in every detail of Shibu Resident, making it an ideal choice for discerning homeowners.
